python

Snakes are elongated, legless reptiles found on every continent except Antarctica. As cold-blooded vertebrates, they regulate their body temperature using their environment. Famous for their flexibility, they use specialized muscles and scales to move. Some snakes use venom to subdue prey, while others rely on constriction.

  • Scientific Name:Python
  • Length: upto 13 meters
  • Lifespan: 20 to 30 years
  • Habitat: Forests & Deserts
